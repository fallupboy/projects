Jeorge Washington

The person who became famous in the whole world

George Washington's Timeline:

  • 1732, FEBRUARY 22 - was born on a plantation called Pope’s Creek, Virginia, British America
  • 1743, APRIL 12 - loses his father
  • 1753, OCTOBER 31 - joins the military in the Ohio Territory. His job was to tell the French troops there to leave
  • 1758, JULY 24 - was elected to represent Frederick County in the Virginia House of Burgesses. The House of Burgesses was part of Virginia’s government
  • 1759, JANUARY 6 - gets married with Martha Custis
  • 1775, JUNE 15 - was chosen to lead the army during the American Revolution. His job was to fight the British Army. The Second Continental Congress gave him the title Commander in Chief.
  • 1776, DECEMBER 25 - led a secret mission on Christmas night. His troops crossed the icy Delaware River. Early in the morning they attacked soldiers fighting for England. The soldiers surrendered. This was a big victory for George Washington.
  • 1789, APRIL 30 - becomes the first President of the United States of America
  • 1792, JANUARY 5 - becomes President of the United States for the second time
  • 1797 - decided it was time for someone else to be president. He had been president for eight years. He returned home to Mount Vernon. John Adams became the second president.
  • 1799, DECEMBER 14 - dies from a throat infection when he was 67 years old. He was buried at Mount Vernon. People mourned his death for many months.
